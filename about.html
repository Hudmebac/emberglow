About Me!

Hi there! I'm Zac, an 8-year-old adventurer! I came up with the idea for Emberglow when I had to create a mythical creature for school. My dad and I teamed up to bring this project to life using cool tools like Co-Pilot and other AI. Together, we made stories, songs, and lots of fun pictures and videos. It's been an amazing journey, and I can't wait for you to explore it with us!

I love exploring new worlds, whether it's in video games or through books. My favorite stories are filled with magic and adventure, and I often imagine myself as a hero in those tales. When I'm not working on Emberglow, you can find me playing Minecraft, where I build incredible structures and go on epic quests with my friends.

I also enjoy drawing and creating my own characters. I like to think about what their stories would be and how they would fit into the worlds I create. My dream is to become a game designer one day, so I can make my own games and share my ideas with others. I believe that creativity has no limits, and I want to inspire other kids to follow their dreams too!

Guess what? I'm also thinking about creating a new character that will be the opposite of Emberglow, inspired by a "Kelpie." Keep an eye out for that, it's coming soon!

Lots of love, Zac Heggie

MineCraft Steve

Bio: A brave adventurer who explores the world of Minecraft. Steve is known for his incredible building skills and resourcefulness. He can gather materials from the environment to create anything from simple homes to complex castles.

With his trusty tools, Steve can mine for precious ores, craft weapons, and defend himself against hostile mobs. His adventurous spirit leads him to explore caves, mountains, and even the Nether, where he faces new challenges and discovers hidden treasures.

Zombie

Bio: A common enemy in Minecraft, known for its relentless pursuit. Zombies are the undead creatures that roam the night, seeking out players to attack. They are slow but persistent, making them a constant threat in the dark.

Despite their fearsome appearance, zombies can be defeated with a little strategy. Players often use torches to light up their surroundings, preventing zombies from spawning. However, if caught off guard, they can quickly overwhelm an unprepared player.

Interestingly, zombies can also pick up items and wear armor, making them even more formidable. They are a reminder to always be vigilant when exploring the world of Minecraft!

Iron Golem

Bio: A protector of villages, strong and loyal. Iron Golems are created by players to defend against hostile mobs. They are massive, powerful beings made of iron blocks, and they will fiercely protect villagers from any threats.

When a player is in danger, Iron Golems will rush to their aid, dealing heavy damage to enemies. Their strength makes them invaluable allies, especially during nighttime when mobs are most active.

In addition to their protective nature, Iron Golems can also be seen wandering around villages, helping to keep the peace. They are a symbol of safety and security in the Minecraft world.

Llama

Bio: A friendly creature that can carry items and spit at enemies. Llamas are unique animals in Minecraft, known for their ability to transport goods across the vast landscapes. They can be equipped with chests, allowing players to carry extra supplies on their adventures.

In addition to their utility, llamas have a quirky personality. They can be seen wandering in herds, and they often make amusing sounds. If threatened, they will spit at their attackers, providing a humorous defense mechanism.

Llamas are also great companions for players who enjoy exploring. Their ability to carry items makes them perfect for long journeys, and they can be led with a lead, making it easy to take them along on adventures.
