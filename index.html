Emberglow

About the Creator

Hi, I am Zac! I am 8 years old, and I came up with the idea for Emberglow when I was tasked with creating a mythical creature at St. Patrick's Primary School in Lochgelly. I started this project with my dad, using images and tools like Co-Pilot and other AI available on the internet. Together, we created stories, songs, and lots of pictures and videos. This has been a fantastic experience, and I have helped my dad bring this idea to life. I hope you enjoy it!

Additionally, I am thinking about creating the opposite and enemy of Emberglow, which will be based on a "Kelpie." Stay tuned for that, coming soon!
